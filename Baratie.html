Alfredo Sauce

Enjoy this classic Italian creamy pasta sauce folded into cooked pasta. It's been adapted over the years to include double cream, making it rich, unctuous, and delicious.

Aubergine & Chilli Tagliolini

Taglioni is a thin, flat ribbon pasta, similar to tagliatelle. If you can't find it, use linguine for this vegetarian pasta dish instead.

Caprese Salad (Tomato and Mozzarella Salad)

Make this classic Italian tricolore salad for a starter or side. It's incredibly simple but, when made with perfectly ripe tomatoes, is a real delight.

Farinata

Make these Italian savoury pancakes using chickpea flour – they're an alternative option for Pancake Day and are served in wedges with a sprinkle of sea salt.

Herby Lamb Fillet with Caponata

Tick off all 5 of your 5 a day with this super-healthy Sunday roast. To make it vegetarian, omit the lamb and add chickpeas to the caponata.

Lasagne Soup

Whip up this easy soup on cold days – it has all the moreish flavours of lasagne, including cherry tomatoes, sheets of pasta, and molten cheese.

Lighter Chicken Cacciatore

The classic Italian 'hunter's stew' gets a healthy makeover, with low-fat chicken breasts, prosciutto, and a rich herby tomato sauce.

Pasta alla Norma

Try this easy version of an alla norma, which uses roast aubergine instead of fried. It's then tossed with spaghetti and a rich tomato and basil sauce.

Spaghetti Puttanesca

Cook up this classic sauce in one pan, then toss with spaghetti for a simple midweek meal. It's budget-friendly too, making it a great meal for the family.

Spinach Crespolini

Enjoy these healthy spelt pancakes filled with spinach, ricotta, and tomato – they deliver all of your 5-a-day, plus spinach supports bone and eye health.

Blackberry Zabaglione

Italian zabaglione is lovely rippled with a few crushed berries for a quick, outstanding dessert.

Strawberry Panna Cotta

Serve this creamy set Italian dessert with a top layer of fresh strawberry sauce and our homemade salted almond snaps on the side.
