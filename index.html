Alfredo Sauce

Thin strands of pasta tossed with roasted aubergine, garlic, and red chilli flakes. Finished with olive oil and Parmesan for a kick of heat and flavor.

Caprese Salad

Fresh buffalo mozzarella, juicy tomatoes, and basil leaves drizzled with extra virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze. A classic Italian starter.

Farinata

A savory pancake made from chickpea flour, olive oil, and rosemary. Crispy on the outside, soft on the inside – a perfect street snack from Liguria.

Herby Lamb Fillet

Juicy lamb fillet seasoned with rosemary and thyme, served with Sicilian-style caponata – a tangy mix of eggplant, olives, and capers.

Lasagne Soup

Lighter Chicken Cacciatore

Pasta alla Norma

Traditional Sicilian dish of pasta with fried eggplant, rich tomato sauce, and salted ricotta cheese, finished with fresh basil.

Spaghetti Puttanesca

Spinach Crespolini

Italian-style crepes filled with spinach and ricotta, baked in a fresh tomato sauce and topped with melted cheese.

Blackberry Zabaglione

Strawberry Panna Cotta

Velvety panna cotta infused with vanilla and topped with a sweet strawberry compote. A beautiful and light dessert to end your meal.

About Us

Welcome to Baratie – where Italian heritage meets heartwarming flavor. Inspired by the rich culinary traditions of coastal Italy and a sprinkle of anime charm, Baratie is more than just a restaurant – it's an experience. From rustic pasta dishes to elegant desserts, each item on our menu is crafted with the freshest ingredients and a love for bold, authentic taste.

At Baratie, we believe food should tell a story. Our chefs draw from centuries of tradition, infusing every plate with passion and creativity. Whether you’re enjoying a romantic evening or grabbing lunch with friends, Baratie’s ambiance and flavors are here to make memories.

Sit back, take a bite, and sail away into the warmth of Italian comfort cuisine. Buon appetito!
